Applendit · applendit@gmail.com Effective: March 23, 2026 Last updated: March 23, 2026

📄 Introduction

Prayer Guide is a free mobile application developed by Applendit (Habil Gür). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

By using Prayer Guide, you agree to the practices described below. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

📱 About the App

Prayer Guide is an Islamic prayer companion that provides:

  • Step-by-step guidance on how to perform daily Salah (5 daily prayers)
  • Wudu (ablution) instructions with illustrations
  • Duas (supplications) in Arabic and transliteration
  • Quranic Surahs (Al-Fatiha, Yasin and others)
  • Illustrated prayer posture guide
  • Essential Islamic knowledge (pillars, fard, wajib, sunnah)
  • Esma-ul Husna — 99 Names of Allah
  • Available in English, Turkish, and German

The app does not require account registration or login and does not collect any personal information to function.

🔍 Information We Collect

Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information. No name, email, phone number, or account credentials are collected or required.

App Content: All prayer content (steps, duas, surahs, postures) is stored locally within the app package and is not transmitted to any server.

Advertising Data (via Google AdMob): The app displays ads through Google AdMob, which may automatically collect the following to serve relevant advertisements:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Android Advertising ID (Ad ID)
  • IP address (used for approximate location for ad targeting)
  • App usage interactions with ads
  • Language and region settings

This data is collected by Google LLC and is governed by Google's own privacy policy. We do not have direct access to or control over this advertising data.

✅ Consent Management (UMP) NEW

In regions where user consent is required by law — including the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and other applicable jurisdictions — the app uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK to request your consent before displaying personalized advertisements.

1
On first launch, a consent form is shown explaining what data may be collected for advertising purposes.
2
You may accept personalized ads, or choose non-personalized (contextual) ads only.
3
Ads are only loaded after your consent decision is recorded. No advertising data is collected before consent.
4
You can withdraw or change your consent at any time through your device settings.

If you are located outside a consent-required region, standard personalized ads may be served without a consent prompt, in accordance with applicable law.

⚙️ App Permissions

Prayer Guide requests only the following permission:

📶 INTERNET — Required to load and display advertisements

We do not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or any other sensitive device permissions.

💾 Local Data Storage

All app content and user preferences (such as selected language) are stored only on your device. We do not operate a backend server that stores your personal data. Nothing is uploaded or synced to a remote server.

👪 Children's Privacy

Prayer Guide is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app is a general-audience Islamic educational app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All ad content is filtered to G-rated material only.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us at applendit@gmail.com and we will promptly address the issue.

🔗 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. The only third-party service integrated is Google AdMob, which acts as an independent data controller for the advertising data it collects and which operates under its own privacy framework as described above.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or to comply with a legal obligation.

🔒 Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our own servers, the risk surface is minimal. All app content is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

For advertising data handled by Google AdMob, security measures are governed by Google LLC.

🌍 GDPR & KVKK Compliance

For users in the European Union (EU) / EEA: We do not process personal data as a data controller. For GDPR purposes, Google acts as an independent data controller for the advertising data it collects via AdMob. Where required by GDPR, we use Google's UMP SDK to obtain your consent before any advertising data collection occurs. You have the right to withdraw consent at any time.

For Turkish users (KVKK): This app complies with the Law on Protection of Personal Data No. 6698. We do not independently process personal data subject to KVKK requirements. Advertising data is handled solely by Google LLC under its own data processing terms.
