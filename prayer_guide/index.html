Features

Everything you need.

🕌
Daily Salah Guide
Step-by-step instructions for all 5 daily prayers — Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib and Isha — with Fard and Sunnah sections.
💧
Wudu Instructions
Illustrated guide to performing ablution correctly before prayer, with detailed descriptions for each step.
🤲
Duas & Supplications
All essential duas used in prayer — Subhanaka, At-Tahiyyat, Allahümme Salli, Allahümme Barik and more — in Arabic and Latin.
📖
Quranic Surahs
Commonly recited Surahs including Al-Fatiha, Yasin and others — with Arabic text and transliteration.
🖼️
Illustrated Postures
Visual guide to every prayer posture — Qiyaam, Ruku, Sujud, Tashahhud — with illustrated references and definitions.
📚
Must-Know Knowledge
Essential Islamic Q&A — the five pillars, fard, wajib, sunnah, conditions of prayer, 99 Names of Allah and more.
🌍
Bilingual — EN & TR
Full support for both English and Turkish. Switch instantly between languages throughout the entire app.
📶
Works Offline
All content is available offline. No internet connection required to learn how to pray.
🔒
No Account Needed
Open and use — no sign-up, no login, no personal data collected. Just the content you need.

Available in

Prayer Guide supports both English and Turkish from the ground up. Every prayer step, dua, surah, posture definition and piece of knowledge is fully translated — so you can learn in your native language.
