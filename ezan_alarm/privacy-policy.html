Privacy Policy

Salah Alarm · Last updated: March 18, 2025 · Developer: Habil Gür / Applendit

This Privacy Policy explains how Applendit ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with the Salah Alarm: Prayer Times application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration or login.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

2. Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location solely to calculate accurate prayer times and to display nearby mosques for your current location. Location data is:

  • Processed locally on your device.
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • Used only while the App is active or when background location is enabled for prayer time updates.

You may revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. The App will continue to function with manually set locations.

3. Notifications

The App uses local notifications to alert you of prayer times. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or data transmission.

4. Internet Access

The App may use an internet connection to fetch updated prayer time data for new locations. No personal data is included in these requests. The App is fully functional offline using cached prayer time calculations.

5. Data Safety (Google Play)

As declared on Google Play:

  • No data is shared with third parties.
  • No personal data is collected by the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for use by all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
